Core Pillars of the Program:

  • Western Musical Theory: Students gain a foundational understanding of harmony, melody, and structure, primarily through the Western system, enabling them to analyze and create complex musical forms.
  • Original Songwriting & Composition: The primary emphasis is on students developing their unique voice by writing their own lyrics, melodies, and instrumental arrangements across various genres.
  • Modern Production & Beat Making: Training includes the use of Digital Audio Workstations (DAW), MIDI sequencing, and sound engineering principles to produce original beats and fully arranged songs.
  • Instrumental Proficiency: While instrumental training may cover traditional elements, the focus is on instruments relevant to global music, such as the piano, guitar, and digital controllers.
