This website is my student project created for the Aesthetics & Technology domain at The Royal Academy (TRA). It's a simple, easy-to-browse guide designed to showcase how we integrate both traditional Bhutanese arts and modern creative technology.

What I Did for This Website

I built several pages that reflect the scope of the Aesthetics curriculum:

  • Traditional Arts Exploration: I created separate pages for different traditional Bhutanese arts, covering techniques like painting, sculpting, weaving, and wood carving.
  • Contemporary Focus: I developed a page dedicated to our modern curriculum, focusing on the Music Program and Technology training.
  • Personal Profile: I included a "Creator" page about myself (Chhimi Dorji) to share my inspirations and connection to the project.

About The Royal Academy (DGI)

The Royal Academy (TRA), under DGI, is a pioneering school that uses the Bhutan Baccalaureate Learning Process. Our education is wholistic, designed to develop students in five key areas: Cerebral, Emotional, Social, Physical, and Spiritual. The Aesthetics & Technology domain ensures we learn to combine the beauty of traditional art with modern innovation.
